# Privacy Policy (Draft)

**Yusra Engineering Ai**

## Data Collection
- **Interaction Data**: We collect system interaction data and conversation history to improve retrieval-augmented generation and system reliability.
- **Media & Voice Biometrics**: Voice inputs used for speaker verification are converted to hashed embeddings. Raw audio for verification is not stored beyond the active session unless explicitly consented for model training.

## Data Retention
- Hot data is retained for 90 days.
- Cold network backups are archived securely.

## User Controls
Users have full rights to request deletion of their conversation history or biometric embeddings via the Settings interface.
